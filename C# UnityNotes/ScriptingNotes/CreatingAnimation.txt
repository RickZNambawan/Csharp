1. Look for sprites (not yet sliced).
2. Go to inspector of the sprites, choose "Multiple" in Sprite Mode and "None" in Compression then click apply.
3. Next, click "Sprite Editor" and sliced all the sprites.
4. Find the sprite (sliced) in the project view and drag all the sprites in the Scene View (or Hierarchy View) and then save your animation in other folder.
5. Delete the object in your scene and find the animation in your project view.
6. After that, open the Animation Window in Window Menu.
7. Look for the animation in the project view and drag it on the Animation window and edit the "Sample".
8. Lastly, put the animation in your player object and put a single sprite in the Sprite Renderer Component.
9. Done.
